Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is laser hair reduction permanent?
Laser significantly reduces hair growth for long-term results. While it may not be 100% permanent for all patients, most experience an 80–95% long-term reduction, making regrowth minimal and fine.
How many sessions are required?
Typically 6–8 sessions are recommended, spaced 4–6 weeks apart, depending on your skin type, hair type, and the area being treated. Our dermatologist will provide a personalized session plan during your consultation.
Is the treatment painful?
Most patients experience only mild warmth or a slight tingling sensation during treatment — far less discomfort than waxing. Our advanced laser systems include cooling technology to ensure maximum comfort.
Is it safe for sensitive skin?
Yes, absolutely. When performed by qualified dermatologists using appropriate laser settings, it is safe for sensitive skin. Our team conducts a thorough skin assessment before each treatment to ensure optimal safety.
What areas can be treated?
We treat virtually any area including the face (upper lip, chin, forehead), arms, underarms, legs, bikini area, back, chest, and more. Schedule a consultation to discuss your specific needs.
Is there any downtime after treatment?
Minimal to no downtime. You may notice slight redness or warmth in the treated area for a few hours, but you can return to your daily activities immediately after the session.
